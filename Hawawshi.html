How to make Hawawshi

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 kg of minced meat
  • 1/2 cup of chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup of chopped green pepper
  • 1/2 cup of chopped tomato
  • 1/2 cup of chopped garlic
  • 1/2 cup of chopped chili

Instructions:

  1. Mix the minced meat with the onion, green pepper, tomato, garlic, and chili.
  2. Season with salt, pepper, and your favorite spices.
  3. Stuff the mixture into pita bread, and bake in the oven until the meat is cooked through.

Notes:

Hawawshi is a delicious Egyptian dish that is perfect for a quick and easy dinner. It is a great way to use up leftover pita bread, and can be made with any type of minced meat. You can also add other vegetables to the mixture, such as carrots, zucchini, or eggplant. For a spicier version, add more chili, or serve with hot sauce on the side.

Enjoy!
