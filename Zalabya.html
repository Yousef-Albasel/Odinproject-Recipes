How to make Zalabya

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • Oil for frying
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour, water, sugar, baking powder, and salt to form a smooth batter.
  2. Heat oil in a deep fryer or a pan to 350°F (175°C).
  3. Dip a spoon into the batter and drop spoonfuls of batter into the hot oil.
  4. Fry until golden brown, turning occasionally for even cooking.
  5. Remove the zalabya from the oil and place them on a paper towel to absorb excess oil.
  6. Dust with powdered sugar before serving.

Notes:

Zalabya is a delicious Middle Eastern dessert. You can also add a touch of cinnamon or orange zest to the batter for extra flavor.

Enjoy your homemade Zalabya!
