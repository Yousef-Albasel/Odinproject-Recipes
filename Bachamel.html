How to make Bashamel

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1/2 cup of flour
  • 4 cups of milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of pepper

Instructions:

  1. Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Stir in the flour until smooth, and cook for about 1 minute.
  3. Gradually whisk in the milk so that no lumps form, and continue cooking and stirring until the mixture thickens.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.

Notes:

This is a basic white sauce, and can be used as a base for many other sauces. For a cheese sauce, add 1 cup of grated cheese to the saucepan after the sauce has thickened. For a curry sauce, add 1 tablespoon of curry powder to the saucepan after the sauce has thickened. For a mustard sauce, add 1 tablespoon of mustard to the saucepan after the sauce has thickened.

Enjoy!

Now that you know how to make bashamel, you can use it to make many other delicious dishes. It is the base for many pasta sauces, and can also be used to make a delicious macaroni and cheese.
